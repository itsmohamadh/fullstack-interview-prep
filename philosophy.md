# 🧠 Philosophy

This repo is intentionally **concise**. It's not a textbook. It's a **launchpad**:

- Use it to identify gaps
- Use it the night before an interview
- Use it to practice quick recall

## How to use this effectively

When you find a topic you don't know:

1. Google it
2. Watch a video
3. Write code
4. Come back here and check it off

## What this repo is NOT

- ❌ A complete JavaScript course
- ❌ A LeetCode replacement
- ❌ A copy-paste cheat sheet for exams

## What this repo IS

- ✅ A map of what actually matters in interviews
- ✅ A quick refresher before the big day
- ✅ A way to track your weak spots

---

**Learn deep. Review quick. Interview well.**
